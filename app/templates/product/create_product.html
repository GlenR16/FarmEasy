List a product

Ready to showcase your product? Whether you're launching something new or expanding your catalog, this is where it starts.
To ensure a smooth and accurate listing, include as much detail as possible
  • The product must have a clear and specific name that accurately represents the item.
  • The product category and type must reflect the nature of the product and its intended use.
  • The product should not be a duplicate of an existing product in your store. Instead of creating a new entry, update the stock of the existing product.
  • The product title or description should not contain any offensive, copyrighted or misleading content.
  • The product should not be a service or a digital product, as we only accept physical products for listing.
  • The product price does not include shipping costs and platform fees, which will be calculated at checkout.
