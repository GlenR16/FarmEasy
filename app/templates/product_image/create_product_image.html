Please follow the image guidelines below to ensure your product listings look professional and comply with our marketplace standards
Image Requirements
  • File Types Allowed: JPG, JPEG, PNG, or WEBP
  • Maximum File Size: 5MB per image
  • Image Dimensions: Minimum 800x800 pixels (square preferred)
  • Number of Images: Up to 6 images per product
Image Quality Guidelines
  • Background: Use a clean, solid white or light-colored background
  • Clarity: Ensure images are sharp and well-lit
  • Framing: Center the product in the frame with minimal cropping
  • No Watermarks or Logos: Avoid adding text, brand logos, or promotional overlays
What to Avoid
  • No inappropriate, offensive, or misleading imagery
  • Avoid including people in the image
  • No borders, graphical elements, or emoji must be present in the images
Legal & Ownership
  • Ensure that you own the rights to the images or have permission to use them
  • The images must accurately represent the product being sold
